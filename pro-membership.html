Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pro membership

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and various local payment methods depending on your region.

Can I switch from monthly to yearly subscription?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to yearly subscription at any time. We'll prorate your remaining monthly subscription and apply it to your yearly plan.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! SEER is designed for students, and we offer special pricing for verified students. Contact our support team with your student ID to learn more about available discounts.

What happens to my content if I downgrade?

Your content remains safe and accessible. However, Pro-exclusive features like live streaming history and pinned posts will no longer be active. You can reactivate them anytime by upgrading back to Pro.

Can I share my Pro subscription?

Pro subscriptions are individual and tied to your account. Each person who wants Pro benefits needs their own subscription. However, we're working on family and group plans!
